Tools to Improve Writing

I was using thesaurus.com for a long time to better my writing. Primarily to spice it with more colorful passages, or make it generally more conforming. In all honesty, formal English makes me struggle; it takes me hours to write a business email or a letter.

To my surprise, that annoying site can be avoided altogether. Ubuntu's apt repository publishes wordnet, extraordinary powerful lexical database; and dict-gcide, allegedly the best English dictionary[1]. 

  sudo apt install wordnet dict-gcide

According to Michael A. Covington's advice, it is important to not use words if you don't know what they mean [2]. Now if I am not confident in an exact sense of the word at hand, wordnet and the dictionary provide tremendous assistance in the matter. dict [word] presents a comprehensive definition with a lot of examples, and wn [word] -over gives an overview of synonym rings.
